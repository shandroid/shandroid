The Order of Elijah

New Line of Defense Lyric Video

Another video for The Order of Elijah, this one incorporates special effects on top of still photography, twitching letters, with a dark and gritty theme of war and death. This was the first video we produced for the band after they signed with Rottweiler Records.

My first adventure with motion design was for Christian deathcore band, The Order of Elijah. The rise in popularity of lyric videos on Youtube sparked their interest in having one produced. I was excited for the opportunity to work on a fresh form of media!
